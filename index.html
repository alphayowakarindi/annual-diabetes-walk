in nairobi

2022

A special day aimed at raising awareness for individuals living with diabetes and raising funds to support the campaign against diabetes from people all over the country.

Main Program

Lecture

Walking

Walk from carnivore grounds past Nyayo Stadium, Uhuru Park, Uhuru Highway, Moi avenue then to Uhuru park

Lecture

Listen from his excellency deputy president and the president of the republic of Kenya.
